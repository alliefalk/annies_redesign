Annie's Parlour's website is a resource offering information about the restaurants menu, location, hours, and reviews for locals and tourists who are seeking more information about the restaurant. Unlike competing restaurants whose websites are hard to navigate, it will be navigable, friendly, and local to Minneapolis through pictures of the atmosphere and notes about its history. Annie's website shows customers the experience of eating at Annie's Parlour.

This classic institution offers classic burger meals in a warm space, plus outdoor seating with city views. We accept credit cards, are kid friendly, have street parking, have outdoor seating, and serve alcohol. Annie's has something on the menu that will make anyone want to come back!

Come to Annie’s to enjoy your favorite flavor milkshake and burger! We’re open late so if you have a late night hankering, stop on in, or place a pick up order online or by calling. We are open 11 am to 11 pm everyday!
